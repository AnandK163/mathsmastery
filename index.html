Meet Anand Kotichintala

With over 34 years of teaching experience, Anand Kotichintala has dedicated his life to making mathematics fun, engaging, and easy to understand for students in grades 6 to 10.

He is the founder of the Math Mastery YouTube channel, where he teaches school-level math and prepares students for competitive exams like TET, POLYCET, NMMS, NTSE, DSC, and more.

Through this website, he aims to create a love for learning mathematics and help students build strong foundations that last a lifetime.
